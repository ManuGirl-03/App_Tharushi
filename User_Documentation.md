## üìã Submitting Repair Requests

### Step-by-Step Booking Process

#### Step 1: Select Your Service
1. Choose the appropriate service from the browse menu
2. Read the service description and pricing
3. Tap **"Select This Service"**

#### Step 2: Add or Select Device
1. **Add New Device:**
   - Tap **"Add New Device"**
   - Enter device details:
     - Device type (e.g., Smartphone, Laptop)
     - Brand (e.g., Samsung, Apple, Dell)
     - Model (e.g., Galaxy S21, MacBook Pro)
     - Serial number (optional)
   - Tap **"Save Device"**

2. **Select Existing Device:**
   - Choose from your saved devices list
   - Tap on the device you want to repair

#### Step 3: Describe the Issue
1. In the **"Issue Description"** field, provide details:
   - When did the problem start?
   - What symptoms are you experiencing?
   - Any recent incidents (drops, spills, etc.)
   - Previous repair attempts
2. **Optional:** Add photos of the device/issue
   - Tap **"Upload Photo"**
   - Select from camera or gallery
   - Add up to 3 photos

#### Step 4: Choose Service Type
**Option A: Home Pickup** (Recommended)
- Our technician will collect your device
- Free pickup within Colombo city limits
- Select preferred pickup time:
  - Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)
  - Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)
  - Evening (6 PM - 8 PM)

**Option B: Service Center Drop-off**
- Bring your device to our service center
- View available centers and their locations
- Select the most convenient center
- Note the operating hours

#### Step 5: Schedule and Confirm
1. **For Home Pickup:**
   - Select preferred date (today + 1 day minimum)
   - Choose time slot
   - Confirm your address

2. **For Drop-off:**
   - View service center details
   - Note operating hours
   - Plan your visit

3. **Review Booking Summary:**
   - Service selected
   - Device information
   - Issue description
   - Service type and schedule
   - Estimated cost
   - Estimated completion time

4. **Submit Request:**
   - Tap **"Submit Booking"**
   - Receive booking confirmation
   - Note your booking ID for tracking

1. Open your booking
2. Tap **"Reschedule"**
3. Select new date and time
4. Confirm changes
5. Receive updated schedule confirmation

## ‚ùì Support and Help

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

#### General Questions

**Q: How long does a typical repair take?**
A: Most repairs are completed within 1-5 business days depending on complexity and parts availability.

**Q: Do you provide warranty on repairs?**
A: Yes, we provide 6 months warranty on all repairs and replaced parts.

**Q: What if my device cannot be repaired?**
A: We'll provide a detailed diagnosis report and won't charge for attempted repairs if the device is deemed irreparable.

**Q: Can I track my repair in real-time?**
A: Yes, you can track your repair status and progress through the mobile app.

#### Service-Specific Questions

**Q: Do you repair water-damaged devices?**
A: Yes, we offer water damage recovery services with varying success rates depending on the extent of damage.

**Q: Can you repair devices still under manufacturer warranty?**
A: We can provide estimates, but repairs may void manufacturer warranty. We recommend checking with the manufacturer first.

**Q: Do you service old/vintage devices?**
A: We service devices up to 10 years old, subject to parts availability.

#### Pricing and Payment

**Q: Are there any hidden costs?**
A: No, all costs are transparently communicated. Any additional charges require customer approval.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Cash, bank transfer, mobile payments, and cards (at service centers).

**Q: Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?**
A: Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. Contact our support team within 7 days of service completion.

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Common App Issues

#### Cannot Login
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify email and password spelling
3. Try "Forgot Password" if needed
4. Clear app cache and restart
5. Contact support if issue persists

#### App Crashes or Freezes
1. Force close and restart the app
2. Restart your device
3. Check for app updates
4. Clear app cache and data
5. Reinstall the app if necessary

#### Cannot Submit Booking
1. Check all required fields are filled
2. Verify internet connection
3. Try reducing photo file sizes
4. Restart app and try again
5. Contact technical support

#### Not Receiving Updates
1. Check notification permissions
2. Verify contact information in profile
3. Check spam/junk folders for emails
4. Ensure SMS notifications are enabled
5. Contact support to verify account status
